The Dementia House Call

The Dementia House Call provides general educational information for caregivers of people living with dementia. It is not medical advice, legal advice, or financial advice, and it is not a substitute for consultation with qualified professionals. Nothing on this site creates a physician–patient relationship. Decisions about an individual's care, including all medication decisions, must be made with that person's own physician or qualified health-care provider. If you believe someone is experiencing a medical emergency, call your local emergency number (911 in Canada and the United States) immediately.
