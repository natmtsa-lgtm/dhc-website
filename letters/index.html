The Dementia House Call provides general educational information for caregivers of people living with dementia. It is not medical advice and is not a substitute for consultation with qualified professionals. Nothing on this site creates a physician–patient relationship. Decisions about an individual's care, including all medication decisions, must be made with that person's own physician. If you believe someone is experiencing a medical emergency, call your local emergency number (911 in Canada and the United States) immediately.
